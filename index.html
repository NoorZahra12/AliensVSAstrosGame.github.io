Background story of our game:

This morning, scientists of the Terran Space Force were picking up signals to see if there was any lifeform across the galaxy.


Suddenly they picked up and decoded certain signal nearby, the presence of Aliens heading towards Earth at a terrifying sight of multiple spaceships inbound was detected.


Commander Tom Lightyear has ordered the Astronauts, including a promising recruit, you are the rookie who must prepare and launch to space with available arms at the table. This will be your first time on duty, in service of the TSF, to destroy all aliens out of this world.

This is your spaceship. You are the rookie assigned to defeat the enemies coming up ahead. Our planet's safety is now in your hands and it is up to you to save our home planet, Earth.
