Meanwhile the operation is on greenlight, and Commander Tom sent his special forces including rookie to continue on to deal with an existential threat. Zurgothrax has sent his General 'Zinister', to finish what the first one could not, for the nearby Space Station has been under attack under his command for the TSF to rescue. General Zinister has been defeated, and the Space Station has been rescued, now as a base of operations for the TSF to plan what seems to be their final assault.


The station's long-range scanners have detected that the Empire's main base of operations is set on Deimos, a moon orbiting on Mars. Rookie and the Astronauts of TSA have come a long way this far, for Emperor Zurgothrax awaits for them, with the strongest force he could ever make, to unleash the full might of his Empire, against the unstoppable TSF. At last, the Battle and Siege of Phobos awaits.



Zurgothrax has been defeated, Phobos has been conquered, and the Alien Empire is no more. Thanks to the efforts of the Terran Space Forces, the Earth was saved from their impending demise. Rookie, the sole Astronaut who carried the counteroffensive on the Empire, came home with the other Astronauts as a hero, and Commander Tom Lightyear has awarded you with the 'Medal of Honor' and the promotion of Commander. And for that, peace is now restored.
